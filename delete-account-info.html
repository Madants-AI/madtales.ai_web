How to Delete Your Account

Step-by-step guide to permanently remove your Madtales AI account

Follow These Steps

Ensure all your data is safely removed

Step 1: Log In

Open the Madtales AI app and log in using your account credentials (email/password or social login).

Step 2: Access Settings

Navigate to the settings or account section of the app where account management options are listed.

Step 3: Delete Account

Select “Delete Account” and follow the prompts. Confirm your action to permanently remove your account and associated data.

Step 4: Contact Support (Optional)

If you face any issues or need confirmation of deletion, contact contact@madtalesai.com.
